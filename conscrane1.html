Usage Tips

1. Ensure the crane is set up on stable ground and properly leveled before operation.
2. Always inspect the crane for any signs of wear or damage before use.
3. Use proper rigging techniques and lifting attachments for different types of loads.
4. Follow all safety protocols and regulations, including load weight limits and crane operation procedures.

FAQs

Q: What type of crane is best suited for lifting heavy materials on construction sites?

A: Tower cranes are commonly used for lifting heavy materials to great heights on construction sites.

Q: Can cranes be operated in windy conditions?
