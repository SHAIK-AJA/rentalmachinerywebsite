Constructional Trenchers

Description: Constructional trenchers are heavy-duty machines used to dig trenches for laying cables, pipes, and drainage systems in construction projects. They are essential for excavating soil efficiently and precisely.

Technical Specifications

  • Dimensions: 120 x 60 x 80 inches
  • Weight: 5,000 lbs
  • Engine Power: 100 HP
  • Trenching Depth: Up to 10 feet
  • Trenching Width: Up to 24 inches

Usage Tips

1. Always inspect the site and mark the trenching path before operating the trencher.
2. Adjust the depth and width settings based on the project requirements.
3. Keep bystanders and workers away from the operating area to prevent accidents.
4. Regularly maintain the trencher by lubricating moving parts and replacing worn-out components.

FAQs

Q: Can the trencher be used in rocky soil conditions?

A: Yes, but it may require special teeth attachments for better performance.

Q: How deep can the trencher dig?

A: The trencher can dig trenches up to 10 feet deep, depending on soil conditions.
